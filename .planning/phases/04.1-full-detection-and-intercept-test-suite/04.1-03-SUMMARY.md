## One-liner

Six Wave 3 E2E integration tests covering file-action T4/T1 paths, clipboard SSN DenyWithAlert, SMB share detection, and OfflineManager fail-closed for unreachable/429 engine scenarios.

Added 6 `#[tokio::test]` functions to `dlp-agent/tests/integration.rs` exercising the complete DLP pipeline from observed event to audit JSONL assertion using the existing `start_mock_engine` infrastructure:
